How to Use Doro Chat

Set Your Name & Color 🎨

Start by entering a fun chat name and picking a color that suits you! This will be your signature color in chat, so make it your style. Hit "Set Name" to lock it in and get ready!

Choose a Room 🏠

Want to explore? Type in a room name in the "Enter room name" box. Hit "Join Room" to hop in, and see the room name at the top of your chat screen.

Send Your Message 💬

Type your message in the chat box, then click "Send" (or press Enter). Links in messages will automatically become clickable, so feel free to share anything cool!

Scroll Through Messages ⬇️

Your chat history is saved locally, so just scroll up anytime to review older messages. And that’s it—dive in, start chatting, and have fun! 🎉

Send Files 📂

Need to share something? You can send files under 800k by pressing the "Send File" button. Stay organized and keep the conversation flowing smoothly!
